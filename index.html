Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion Process

Advanced extruders with precise temperature control ensure consistent melt flow and optimal pipe dimensions throughout the production run.

  • Temperature-controlled zones
  • Consistent melt pressure

Controlled Cooling System

Rapid and uniform cooling in water tanks ensures dimensional stability and optimal crystalline structure for maximum performance.

  • Uniform cooling profile
  • Stress-free solidification

Vacuum Sizing Technology

Precision sizing tanks maintain exact outer diameter specifications while internal air pressure ensures perfect roundness.

  • ±0.1mm diameter tolerance
  • Perfect ovality control

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes comprehensive dimensional, pressure, and material testing to ensure compliance with international standards.

  • 100% dimensional inspection
  • Hydrostatic pressure testing

Permanent Product Marking

Inkjet printing systems apply permanent identification markings including size, pressure rating, standards compliance, and batch traceability.

  • ISO-compliant marking
  • Full batch traceability

Precision Cutting & Length Control

Automated cutting systems deliver precise pipe lengths with clean, square ends ready for immediate installation.

  • ±5mm length tolerance
  • Clean, burr-free cuts

Secure Packaging & Dispatch

Protective packaging ensures pipes arrive in perfect condition. Coils are wound on wooden reels, straight lengths are bundled and strapped.

  • Weather-resistant packaging
  • Safe transport guarantee
